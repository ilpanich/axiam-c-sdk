1/*
2 * AXIAM C SDK — Framework-agnostic route guard (§10) and declarative
3 * authorization helpers (§11).
4 *
5 * These compose strictly on top of the §10 authentication path: the guard
6 * verifies the request's session token via JWKS (§ JWKS), then performs the
7 * §11 authorization check for the *authenticated caller* (subject propagation,
8 * §11.2). No decision caching (§11.6). Network failure fails CLOSED (503).
9 *
10 * The macros AXIAM_REQUIRE_ACCESS / AXIAM_REQUIRE_AUTH / AXIAM_REQUIRE_ROLE are
11 * C's analog of the annotations other SDKs expose (§11).
12 */
